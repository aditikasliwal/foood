I dont like to take myself or my practice too seriously. Yoga is fun as well as extremely beneficial and I try to encourage my students to approach every class with an attitude of self-love, curiosity, playfulness and joy. Yoga is self-love - learning the practices to take care of all areas of our health. So often, we consider yoga to be a physical practice but when we are open to all the elements of yoga, we're able to work deeply on every layer of ourselves: the physical, mental, emotional, spiritual and energetic. Shedding layers to unveil new versions of us over and over again.
